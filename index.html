A Few Words About Us

We're United From Almost 0 Years

The Batch 23 is a non profit group by alumni students of JNV Solapur 23rd batch students. We do not value the pay, We value your feelings!

The motto of this group is to stay connected besides from the metres of distances. And make every memory og your 5yrs of JNV immortal forever. Although all of you have been advised to follow all the social media handles of batch23.official to be a part of group.

In case in the future, we planned a #Reunion ceremony then you all be tuned by this group.
